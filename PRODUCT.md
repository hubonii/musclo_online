# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Dedicated strength athletes, hypertrophy-focused lifters, and fitness enthusiasts who value scientific training methodologies and data-driven progress. These are users who want to see their "Biological Reality" reflected in high-fidelity logs.

## Product Purpose
To transform raw workout data into a strategic roadmap for physical excellence. Musclo bridges the gap between simple logging and intelligent coaching by providing contextual audits, 1RM estimations, and anatomical heatmaps.

## Brand Personality
Precise, Authoritative, and Tactile. The brand feels like a high-end engineering tool for the human body—scientific yet accessible, high-performance (via its True Blue signature), and physically grounded through Neumorphic depth.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS Blue**: No "enterprise-safe" corporate palettes. Our Blue (#0000EE) is high-voltage and industrial.
- **Flat UI**: Avoiding the "paper-thin" look; everything should have mass and depth.
- **AI Slop**: No generic chatbots or "magic" icons without substance.
- **Cluttered Fitness Grids**: No overwhelming tables of numbers without visual hierarchy.

## Design Principles
- **Tactile Intelligence**: Every interactive element should feel like a physical button or dial on an elite piece of equipment.
- **Scientific Authority**: Use precise scales, formulaic labels (like Brzycki), and anatomical accuracy to build trust.
- **High-Integrity Performance**: Animations should be snappy and purposeful, reflecting the intensity of the training itself.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **High Contrast**: Ensuring the True Blue accent maintains visibility against light and dark surfaces.
- **Reduced Motion**: Respecting system preferences for users sensitive to animations.
- **Large Tap Targets**: Optimizing for users who may be tracking workouts with sweaty or shaky hands.
